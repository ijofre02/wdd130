EXITING EXPEDITIONS COMPANY

Our "purpose" is to bring happiness to families and allow everyone to have an exciting experience. For that, we rely on high safety technology and equipment. We believe that an exciting experience has incalculable value and everyone should participate. That's why our prices are cheap and accessible. Our motto is: "Have fun safely" — fear is not an excuse.

History

Our company started in 1982 when a group of friends began a tradition of going on whitewater rafting adventures. Over time, each one formed a family and introduced them to the tradition. So, our first concern was keeping

our families safe without missing the exciting rafting experience. That's how, without even realizing it, we started a small company that improved over time until we became "EXITING EXPEDITIONS", with a bunch of experience in this...
